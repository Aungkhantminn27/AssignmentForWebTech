Part3: University policies and services

PLAGIARISM
Plagiarism is treated seriously. If we suspect any work is copied, all the students are likely to recieve zero for the entire assignment. Plagiarism has led the students being expelled from the University.
Please click here to read more about plagiarism.

ACADEMIC CONSIDERATION
We know that life isn't perfect and there may be a time when you can't hand in the assignment on time, or make it to a class or exam. If you find yourself in a situation like this, you may be eligible for Acadic Consideration. Academic consideration is designed to help when they are sick, injured, or where a serious, unplanned situation has occured that has affacted their ability to study.
Please click here to read more about plagiarism.
